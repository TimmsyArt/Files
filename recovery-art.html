Escape from the Clutches of Addiction

Mixed Media

This image represents the moment of breaking free. The hands gripping the head and pulling backward symbolise addiction's constant pull—its pressure, control, and refusal to let go.

This image represents the moment of breaking free. The hands gripping the head and pulling backward symbolise addiction's constant pull—its pressure, control, and refusal to let go. The figure turning forward and opening their mouth reflects an act of resistance: a choice to speak, to breathe, and to move in a different direction. The words "This way to escape the clutches of addiction" point toward hope, though not an easy escape—one that demands effort, pain, and determination. It captures a transition in which addiction is still present but no longer in control, and where reclaiming one's voice and direction marks the first step toward a life without addiction.

About This Collection

This collection will feature artwork inspired by themes of recovery, hope, and personal transformation. Each piece will explore the journey of healing and the power of resilience.
