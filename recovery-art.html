Escape from the Clutches of Addiction

Mixed Media

From my perspective, this image represents the moment of breaking free. The hands gripping my head and dragging me backward symbolise addiction's constant pull—its pressure, control, and refusal to let go. Turning my face forward and opening my mouth is an act of resistance: a choice to speak, to breathe, to move in a different direction. The words "This way to escape the clutches of addiction" point toward hope, but not an easy escape—one that requires effort, pain, and determination. It captures the transition where addiction is still close behind, but no longer in control, and where reclaiming my voice and direction becomes the first step toward a life without it.

About This Collection

This collection will feature artwork inspired by themes of recovery, hope, and personal transformation. Each piece will explore the journey of healing and the power of resilience.
